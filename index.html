⚠️ Understanding Risk — Please Read

A 1% daily target means risking your capital to potentially earn 1% per day. This is the easiest target to achieve and carries the lowest risk. As you move up tiers, the potential daily gain increases — but so does the difficulty and the risk of loss.

5% is harder than 1%. 10% is harder than 5%. Each tier increase multiplies both your earning potential AND your risk of losing money. The most consistently successful members trade at 1-5% tiers.

These are potential returns only — not guaranteed. Forex trading involves substantial risk. You can lose all your deposited capital. Never deposit money you cannot afford to lose.
